Embark on a breathtaking journey to Mount Titlis and the charming city of Lucerne, guided by our expert Travelxploria representative and the best on-ground support staff. Begin by exploring Lucerne's historic Old Town, Chapel Bridge, and the Lion Monument, immersing yourself in the city's rich history. Then, ascend into the heart of the Swiss Alps, where you'll ride the Rotair revolving cable car to 3,020 meters (9,908 feet). Once at the top, experience the Glacier Cave, Ice Flyer Chairlift, and Europe's highest suspension bridge, the Cliff Walk.
