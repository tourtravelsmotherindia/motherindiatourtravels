After settling in, take some time to relax before stepping out for Delhi sightseeing and exploring nearby local markets for shopping, culture, and authentic street food. Explore the captivating sights of Delhi on your Golden Triangle Tour

On the second day of your 7-Day Delhi Agra Jaipur Tour Package, enjoy a delicious breakfast before setting out to discover the highlights of Delhi sightseeing. Guided by our expert team, you will visit iconic landmarks such as the India Gate war memorial, Parliament House, President’s House, and the Secretariat Building, along with spiritual sites like Jama Masjid and the Lotus Temple.


No Golden Triangle India Tour is complete without exploring the historic Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Raj Ghat, and Shanti Vana — monuments that showcase Delhi’s rich heritage. After an enriching day of sightseeing in Delhi, return to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Be mesmerized by the historic wonders of Agra

On the third day of your journey, enjoy a hearty breakfast before checking out from your hotel. You will be transferred from Delhi to the historic city of Agra, a magnificent Mughal-era destination in Uttar Pradesh. Upon arrival, check into your hotel to freshen up before visiting the iconic Taj Mahal—one of the world’s wonders, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in loving memory of his wife, Queen Mumtaz Mahal.

In addition to the Taj Mahal, explore the impressive Agra Fort, the former main residence of the Mughal emperors. This tour offers a rich cultural experience filled with history and stunning architecture. After a memorable day, return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site En Route to Jaipur

After enjoying your morning breakfast, check out from the hotel and begin your journey towards Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan. En route, you’ll have the opportunity to explore some fascinating historical landmarks, including Jama Masjid, Diwan-e-Aam, Buland Darwaza, the Tomb of Salim Chisti, and Panch Mahal. A major highlight is Fatehpur Sikri, a UNESCO World Heritage Site built by Emperor Akbar, showcasing the grandeur of Mughal architecture.

After an enriching stopover, continue your journey to Jaipur. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax for an overnight stay.

Discover the Royal Charm of the Pink City

On the fifth day of your journey, start your morning with a delightful breakfast before setting out to explore Jaipur, famously known as the Pink City. Begin your sightseeing with a visit to the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), followed by the Jantar Mantar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its ancient astronomical instruments. Continue to the majestic City Palace, a grand architectural marvel that reflects the royal heritage of Jaipur.

As you explore the city, immerse yourself in its vibrant culture—shop for traditional Rajasthani attire, colorful footwear, junk jewelry, and exquisite handicrafts. After a culturally enriching day, return to your hotel for a restful overnight stay.

Explore the Sacred Cities of Pushkar and Ajmer

On the sixth day of your tour, enjoy a delicious breakfast and check out from the hotel as you set off for a day trip to Ajmer and Pushkar. Upon arrival, you will be warmly welcomed and guided by our Travelxploria representative. Begin your visit with the revered Dargah Sharif, the tomb of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti, a major pilgrimage site attracting devotees from all over the world.

Continue your journey to Pushkar, known for its spiritual aura and scenic charm. Here, you’ll visit the Brahma Temple, one of the few temples dedicated to Lord Brahma. After exploring the spiritual and cultural highlights of these sacred cities, return to Jaipur for an overnight stay at your hotel.

Cherishing the Final Day of a Memorable Journey

On the final day of your incredible journey, enjoy a hearty breakfast before packing your bags. After check-out from the hotel, our Travelxploria Representative will assist you with a smooth transfer to the Jaipur Airport/Railway Station for your return journey home. Depart with a heart full of unforgettable memories and experiences from this enriching trip.
